CVC

Computation and Visualization Consortium

    The CVC formed during the preparation of proposals for the 2012 “College’s science education” grant program sponsored by the Howard Hughes Medical Institute (HHMI). Six institutions (listed below) created the CVC with the goal of providing workshops for faculty to enhance their capacity to use increasingly complex and large data sets in their research and classrooms.

    Faculty from non-CVC institutions are welcome to participate as well.

    Teaching Resources
